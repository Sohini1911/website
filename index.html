Sohini Gupta

Namaskar! I’m an incoming PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Alberta and Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii) , starting Winter 2026. I completed my Dual Degree in Electrical Engineering (B.Tech) and Machine Learning and Signal Processing (M.Tech) from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur .

My doctoral research will be conducted in the NeuBahar Lab under the supervision of Dr. Bahareh Tolooshams . My focus will be on NeuroAI, with interests in representation learning, inverse problems, and interpretable AI inspired by the brain. I also plan to explore Brain-Computer Interface applications, with a wish to create impact that serves humanity.

Outside of research, I am passionate about sports, and I have a love for badminton and volleyball. I also enjoy baking and have a decent hand at painting (view) .
